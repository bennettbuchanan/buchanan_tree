Welcome to the Buchanan/McBride family web. This is a long-term project to catalog the history, correspondence, and images of individuals and places related to the marriage of James M. Buchanan and Lillian McBride and their ancestral families. For privacy reasons, this site will not be cataloged or indexed on the World Wide Web.

About the images. When possible, the date of the image and type of medium (e.g., tintype, daguerrotype) will be noted. The individuals in some of the photos are unknown. If you have any information on who that person might be, please send it along to me. Also, if you have something you would like to contribute, please send that along as well. If you can scan your image and save it as a jpeg or gif file, that would be best. If you want to send me the original, I will scan it and return it to you. ..
