[4 x 3 inch paper note enclosed in daguerrotype of Elizabeth Roger -- held by Nancy P. Buchanan, July 30, 2006]

"April 7, 55

Dear. Bill and Nancy. This is a De Gerry-type Photo of your dear--Great Grandmother Elizabeth Rogers, who, was my G-mother and wife of my G-father Henry-J. Rogers. Who helped to perfect the first Telegraph. The magnetic between Balto and Washington D.C. also [      ] Superintende of this Co. Jim Jr. can give you more details about this. As he as the data. This is very bad writing but the best I can do under the circumstances.

This picture is a little faded but it can be brought back the way it was a hundred years ago. So, Al said the cost of these pictures at time of taking was high." $50.00 and $75.00 each. Yes your dear great G-father had dough in those days--a grand old man. Dad B."

[To Bill and Nancy Buchanan]
