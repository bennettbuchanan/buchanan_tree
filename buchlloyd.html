Lloyd Buchanan

7. LLOYD7 BUCHANAN (GEORGE6, MUNGO5, MUNGO4, ROBERT3, MUNGO2, ROBERT1) was born 10 November 1729 in St. Paul's Parish, Baltimore Co., Maryland, and died 1761 in Druid Hill, Baltimore Co., Maryland. He married (1) ELEANOR DANNALL 1749. She died Bef. 1757. He married (2) REBECCA LAWSON 14 July 1757, daughter of ALEXANDER LAWSON and DOROTHY SMITH. She was born Bef. 1742, and died Unknown.
