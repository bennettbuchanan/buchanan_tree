Notes:

Admitted to the Signet as a Writer in 1695. In 1709, he purchased an estate known as Auchintorlie in the parish of Old Kilpatrick, Dumbartonshire -- to the west of Glasgow. Auchintorlie was sold in 1737 to Andrew Buchanan of Drumpellier.

Mungo Buchanan's testament is dated 19 April 1711.
