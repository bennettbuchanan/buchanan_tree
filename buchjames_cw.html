Nov. 23, 1861 to ? Civil War service—Third Maryland Artillery

Reference 1 Third Maryland Artillery (later Ritter's Battery, Georgia Light Artillery)
Reference 2 National Archives Military Records notes
Reference 3 Organization of the Army of Tennessee (see Johnston's Battalion, Capt. John B. Rowan, Stephens (Georgia) Light Artillery, Lieut. William L. Ritter (Nov. 14, 1864 to Jan. 23, 1865)
Reference 4 Third Battery Maryland Artillery, Vicksburg Monument, NPS

Joined Thomas' Legion (North Carolina) following the fall of Vicksburg, July 4, 1863.

Commissioned First Lieutenant, September 30, 1862. In Thomas' Legion, Walker's Regiment, Company 2nd D. Entire company subsequently transferred to Love's Regiment as Co. D. (www.bcooper.aaahosting.net/page17).

Captured February 2, 1864 and eventually sent to Fort Delaware south of Philadelphia. Listed on rolls of prisoners there (See, Prisoner of War Records below).
