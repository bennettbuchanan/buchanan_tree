12. WILLIAM7 BUCHANAN (GEORGE6, MUNGO5, MUNGO4, ROBERT3, MUNGO2, ROBERT1) was born 07 July 1748 in St. Paul's Parish, Baltimore Co., Maryland, and died 19 December 1824. He married (1) MARGARET DORSEY Bef. 1800. She died Unknown. He married (2) HEPHZIBAH BROWN Aft. 1800. She was born in New Jersey, and died Unknown.

Notes for HEPHZIBAH BROWN:
Widow of Moulden Perine. Mother of David Perine of "Homeland"
