Dorsey family history

27. Caleb Dorsey was born on 18 Jul 1710 in Anne Arundel County, Maryland and died in Jun 1772 in Anne Arundel County, Maryland.

Caleb married Priscilla Hill on 10 Feb 1734 in Anne Arundel County, Maryland.

Children from this marriage were:
