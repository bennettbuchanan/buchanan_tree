GEORGE S. BUCHANAN -7 BOHANAN (GEORGE6 BUCHANAN, MUNGO5, MUNGO4, ROBERT3, MUNGO2, ROBERT1) was born 14 August 1738 in St. Paul's Parish, Baltimore Co., Maryland, and died 1797 in St. Mary's Co., Maryland. He married ELIZABETH HOLLAND 1761. She died 1798 in St. Mary's Co., Maryland.
