Susan Terese Rogers
(1844-1875)

Father: Henry J. Rogers (d. 1879)

Mother: Elizabeth Ann Maglenen (daughter of James Maglenen and Susanna T. Lea of Baltimore--http://memory.loc.gov/ll/llsb/035/llsb035.txt)

Vitals:

  • Born: January 6, 1844, "born at the residence of her Grandmother Rogers in Columbia St. on Saturday evening January 6th 1844." [Rogers family bible]
  • Place of birth: Baltimore, Maryland
  • Died: March 12, 1875 "at about 6 p.m." [Rogers family bible]
  • Place of death: "at the [Rogers] family residence, 160 N. Howard Street" [Rogers family bible]
  • Buried: Greenmount Cemetary, Baltimore
